Here's timeline of Elon Musk life :

  • 1971 - Elon Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1980 - Elon Musk's parents divorced.
  • 1981 - Elon Musk started becoming interested in computing with the Commodore VIC-20 and even taught himself computer programming.
  • 1983 - By age 12, Elon Musk sold his first ever game "Blastar" to a magazine that was called PC and Office Technology for $500.
  • 1989 - Elon Musk, age 17, had to move to Canada in order to attend Queen's University. He planned to go to the United States and it was easier coming from Canada.
  • 1992 - Elon Musk studied Business and Physics at the University of Pennsylvania graduating with an undergraduate degree in economics and was able to stay for a second bachelor's degree in studying physics.
  • 1995 - Elon Musk started an internet city guide web software company Zip2 with his brother Kimbal.
  • 1999 - Zip2 was sold to Compaq for $307 million USD.
  • 1999 - Elon Musk cofounded X.com with $10 million coming from the sale of Zip2
  • 2000 - X.com merged with Confinity in order to form PayPal. Elon Musk married his first wife Justine Wilson whom he met at Ontario's Queen's University. Elon Musk lost his infant son Nevada Alexander Musk at 10 weeks old.
  • 2000 - Elon Musk got fired as CEO of PayPal over his desire for PayPal's Unix-based infrastructure to be moved to Microsoft Windows.
  • 2001 - Elon Musk started conceptualizing the Mars Oasis in an attempt to raise public interest in space exploration.
  • 2001 - Elon Musk travelled to Moscow, Russia to buy refurbished Dnepr Intercontinental ballistic missiles or ICBMs which could send potential payloads into space. Elon Musk, however, went back to the United States empty-handed.
  • 2002 - Elon Musk decided to build affordable rockets by starting a company.
  • 2002 - Elon Musk founded SpaceX with $100 million USD of his own project becoming CEO and CTO.
  • 2002 - eBay bought PayPal for $1.5 billion USD. Elon Musk got $165 million.
  • 2003 - Tesla had become incorporated.
  • 2004 - Elon Musk's wife gave birth to two sons, twins, through in vitro fertilization.
  • 2004 - Elon Musk joined Tesla's board of directors as Chairman leading the Series A round of investment.
  • 2006 - Elon Musk's wife gave birth to three sons through in vitro fertilizations.
